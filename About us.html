How Kungu Bakery Uganda Limited started

The bakery was founded by a team of zealous men who were then students of Uganda Technology And Management University Kungu Campus back in 2022 licenced under the private company act of the parliament of uganda with licence number A202201-WAK-UG and was certified by the uganda National Bureau of Standards on 17-01-2022
Due to the limited services that were around the area by the time, with their great business knowledge and willingness to serve the country by offering services to the citizens, these great men came up with the idea of starting a bakery in the area which they went on to implement at the start of the year 2022.
At the beginning, due to limited capital,they started with yellow bread ,brown bread,and birthday cakes
Due to proper management,at the end of the year 2022 the company had realised huge profits that it had to build its own structures and moved from the rented houses. The company started producing more products that included Madiera cakes,party cakes,swirl bread plus other bread varieties and cookies.
